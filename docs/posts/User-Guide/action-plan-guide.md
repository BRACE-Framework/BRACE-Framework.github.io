3. ### WHAT WE WILL DO INSTEAD 

* Normal way of working (before disruption):\_\_\_\_  
* Fallback during disruption:\_\_\_\_  
* Last resort if fallback fails:\_\_\_\_

4. ### WHO DOES WHAT 

* Who activates the fallback plan?  
* Who takes over if they are unreachable?  
* How will the team know the plan is active?

5. ### WHAT WE NEED 

This plan depends on:

* Existing tools  
* Specific people  
* External partners  
* Physical access (office, devices, travel)  
* Anything fragile or uncertain?

6. ### SAFETY CHECK 

* Does this plan increase risk to anyone? No / Yes / Unsure  
* If yes or unsure, how will we reduce risk?

7. ### REVIEW 

* When will we review or update this plan?  
* Who is responsible for updating it?

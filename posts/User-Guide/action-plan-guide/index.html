    BRACE ACTION PLAN (SIMPLE VERSION)

    Purpose -Prepare for one likely Internet disruption by agreeing in advance on what to do and who does it.

    1. FOCUS

    Disruption scenario:

    Main priority during disruption:

    1. WHAT MUST CONTINUE

    What is the minimum that must keep working? (e.g. one daily update, coordination between 2–3 people)

    1. WHAT WE WILL DO INSTEAD

    2. Normal way of working (before disruption):____

    3. Fallback during disruption:____

    4. Last resort if fallback fails:____

    5. WHO DOES WHAT

    6. Who activates the fallback plan?

    7. Who takes over if they are unreachable?

    8. How will the team know the plan is active?

    9. WHAT WE NEED

    This plan depends on:

    • Existing tools
    • Specific people
    • External partners
    • Physical access (office, devices, travel)

    • Anything fragile or uncertain?

    • SAFETY CHECK

    • Does this plan increase risk to anyone? No / Yes / Unsure

    • If yes or unsure, how will we reduce risk?

    • REVIEW

    • When will we review or update this plan?

    • Who is responsible for updating it?
